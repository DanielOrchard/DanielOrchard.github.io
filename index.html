About Daniel

Leading Zenith Digital, Daniel stands out as an Unreal Engine development specialist, known for his practical approach to solving complex problems and creating innovative solutions. With a solid background in C++, Blueprint, and Editor Scripting, Daniel leverages his expertise across various mediums including film, TV, and video games, establishing himself as a versatile and indispensable asset to any project.

Daniel's dedication to delivering practical and effective solutions is evident in his contributions to high-profile projects such as Game of Thrones (Virtual Production), House of the Dragon (Pre-Vis VP), The Mandalorian (Tools Dev), Avengers: Age of Ultron (Performance Capture), and RSC's Dream 2021 (Lead Developer), among others (See past projects for a full history). His knack for developing new features, tools, and integrations that are both robust and user-friendly has solidified his reputation as a solutions expert within the Unreal Engine community.

Beyond his project involvement, Daniel is passionate about education and knowledge sharing, offering tutorials and courses on various online platforms to empower the next generation of Unreal Engine developers.

Daniel's grounded nature and emphasis on delivering practical, tailored solutions position him as the ideal partner for a wide range of projects, from blockbuster productions to innovative technological ventures. His commitment to excellence and innovation ensures that each collaboration is not merely a service, but a forward leap in Unreal Engine development.

Daniel has a strong preference for engaging in project-based work, valuing the opportunity to come on board for specific projects where he can apply his problem-solving skills and technical expertise to produce tangible results. This approach allows him to focus deeply on each project's unique challenges, ensuring that he delivers high-quality, innovative solutions tailored to the project's needs. Daniel's dedication to this mode of work reflects his commitment to delivering excellence and value in every endeavor.
